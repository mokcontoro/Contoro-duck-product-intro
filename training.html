Contoro at a Glance

Our Technology

This is our Autonomous Trailer Unloading Robot — also known as the Contoro Duck. It automates one of the most physically demanding tasks in warehouse logistics.

What Do We Do?

We automate offloading floor-loaded trailers. Contoro Duck enters the container, picks up boxes one at a time, and places them onto the conveyor system for palletization.

Why This Matters

Reduces injury risk for associates and allows them to work on safer tasks.

Keeps associates inside the warehouse and out of extremely hot or cold containers.

Safety

Safety & Reliability

Built for Safety

Contoro Duck is designed with multiple layers of industrial safety:

  1. High Voltage Isolation — Fully enclosed 480V power system with safety interlocks
  2. Protected Moving Parts — Conveyors and drive mechanisms behind safety barriers
  3. Controlled Robotic Arm — KUKA arm with multi-zone safety monitoring

Comprehensive Safety Features

  1. Status Indicator Light — Clear visual status for operational awareness
  2. KUKA Arm Monitoring — Real-time force and position monitoring
  3. Electrical Safety — Fully enclosed panels with lockout/tagout
  4. Environmental Awareness — Designed for busy warehouse environments
  5. 6 Emergency Stops — Redundant E-stops throughout the system

Smart Exception Handling

Contoro Duck handles edge cases with built-in response protocols:

  1. Collision Detection — Immediate stop on unexpected contact
  2. Stall Recovery — Automatic reporting and remote support integration
  3. Zone Monitoring — Instant shutdown if unauthorized entry detected
  4. Anomaly Response — Immediate halt on unexpected movement patterns

Easy Teardown

Teardown is just as simple — reverse the four steps:

1

Safety Fence

Retract and stow

2

Conveyors

Disconnect and collapse

3

Robot / Duck

Drive back to storage (Yellow mode)

4

Safety Cart

Return to storage position

Simple 6-Step Operation

I

Setup

Position cart, drive Contoro Duck to container, set up conveyors, deploy fence

II

Assign Container

Choose Facility and Container ID in the UI

III

Start Robot

Click Green Start Button on the Safety Cart

IV

Monitor & Respond

Pause from UI, Reset from Safety Cart if Blue, E-Stop for emergencies

V

Unloading Finished

Robot auto-detects empty container and stops

VI

Teardown

Reverse setup: fence, conveyors, Contoro Duck to storage, cart
